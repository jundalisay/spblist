We propose a Modified Periodic Tables of Elements that replaces the names of elements with their atomic numbers.

This is because Material Superphysics will open up new ways to create elements, leading to more elements. 

This will make the old system very inconvenient.


{{< i s="/graphics/physics/periodic.png" a="Simplified Periodic Table" >}}

In our system for example, Sodium Hydroxide (NaOH) will be notated as `e11_e08_e01` pronounced "eleven-eight-one". 

It disolves in water `2e01_e08` pronounced "one-duo-eight" to form:
- `e11(1som)` 
- `e08_e01(1sor)`

In this way, people don't need to memorize "Sodium", "Hydroxide", etc.
